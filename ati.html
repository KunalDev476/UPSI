About the Program

What is the Innovation Resource Hub?

The Innovation Resource Hub is a one-stop shop center for all ATI consortia members to access resources they need to thrive. We believe true innovation originates from teaming and collaboration. And, in order for innovators to be successful, they need a healthy, well-resourced ecosystem.
