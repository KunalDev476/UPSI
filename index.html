About govmates

A Federal Technology Ecosystem Built on Trust

Govmates is a technology scouting platform. We merge the best practices of technology and the human element to promote non-traditional defense contractors within the federal community.

We operate a managed ecosystem of over 8,000 non-traditional federal contractors, facilitating matches between innovative companies and U.S. Federal Government customers — from market research to technology transition.

What We Do

Managing a 8,000+ Member Ecosystem

Govmates operates a technology scouting platform managing an ecosystem of over 8,000 non-traditional federal contractors, industry partners, academic institutions, research organizations, and government stakeholders focused on innovative solutions for U.S. Federal Government needs. We provide an enterprise-level approach to identifying, developing, and transitioning technologies, facilitating over 41,000 matches for U.S. Federal Government customers and industry partners supporting market research, non-traditional outreach, and teaming.

Technology Scouting Tool

Rapidly identifies desired technologies using unbiased algorithms and relevance scoring, applying formulaic and methodical approaches to solving U.S. Federal Government challenges.

Collaborative Matchmaking Events

The bridge between Government and Industry — maintaining extensive relationships with non-traditional contractors, large systems integrators, defense primes, and commercial enterprises generating 39,000+ successful matches.

Valley of Death Combat

Resources, training, and education helping members build DCAA-compliant operations for FAR-based federal contracts, supporting scalable growth and profitability for firms navigating government contracting.

Our Approach

Six Pillars of Enterprise-Level Support

Govmates leverages extensive reach and diverse membership to deliver enterprise solutions for technology identification and transition. The organization has enabled partner growth across the country through matchmaking services that have proven valuable to startups and smaller government contracting businesses.

01

Access to Innovators and Non-Traditionals

The platform hosts over 5,000 change-makers developing innovative mission-supporting solutions, linking members with expertise and targeted support to address complex problems through an extensive network.

02

High Touch Collaboration

Govmates provides concierge-level matchmaking, making introductions meaningful and facilitating progression from initial conversations through NDAs and beyond, serving as a catalyst for exponential growth.

03

Unbiased Algorithm

Operating as an honest broker and transparent intermediary, maintaining no equity stakes in member companies. Matching decisions focus exclusively on what benefits the business involved.

04

Valley of Death Combat

Understanding resource constraints facing government contracting firms, govmates provides training, education, and resources helping members build DCAA-compliant operations for FAR-based federal contracts.

05

Valley of Death Resources

Recognizing struggles with business longevity, the organization addresses common Valley of Death challenges, helping small businesses overcome initial obstacles and develop into formidable enterprises.

06

Enterprise Level Solution

Delivering comprehensive solutions connecting industry and government with innovative small businesses, ensuring partnerships prove profitable and amicable while pairing stakeholders based on mutual needs and capabilities.

Enterprise Level Solution

A Full Partnership for Government & Industry

Govmates delivers comprehensive solutions connecting industry and government with innovative small businesses, ensuring partnerships prove profitable and amicable while pairing stakeholders based on mutual needs and capabilities.
