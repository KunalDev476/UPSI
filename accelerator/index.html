Program Journey

Govmates facilitates Elevate through:

Education

Participants will receive a tailored course of education determined by their goals and positioning within the GovCon Lifecycle.

Mentorship

Govmates and their partners are uniquely capable of guiding cohort members through the US procurement landscape.

Business Matching

Govmates' matchmaking algorithms will match cohort companies to representatives in Federal Agencies and System Integrator companies based upon the capabilities provided in their Govmates profile.

Community Growth

Cohort members have access to a community portal for collaboration and resource access.

Industry Resources

As part of the Govmates ecosystem, cohort members will have continued access to additional networking and matchmaking events, industry resources and introductions, and education sessions as they continue to grow.

Networking

Cohort members will work with the Govmates team alongside their SME industry resource partners to develop US Federal pitch strategies, capture and opportunity planning, and growth trajectory to create a company-specific Federal Market strategy they can implement during and beyond the completion of the accelerator program.
